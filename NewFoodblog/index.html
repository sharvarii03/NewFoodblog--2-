Strawberry & Greens Salad

  • Type

    Veg

  • Persons

    1
Ingredients:
2-3 cups of mixed lettuce chopped, 1 cup sliced strawberries, 1 cup diced cucumbers, 2 toasted bread (croutons), 1 tbsp roasted pumpkin seeds, 1 tbsp roasted melon seeds For the dressing, 1/2 cup whisked yogurt, 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tsp sugar

Method :

1. Wash and chop mixed lettuce leaves; dice some cucumbers and then slice the strawberries to your preferance.
2. Slice some bread(fresh or leftover) and chop the bread into small cubes and air fry(or toast) it.
3. Mix the prepared ingredients into a bowl.
4. For the dressing add sugar, balsamic vinegar and a pinch of salt to whisked yogurt and pour the dressing over the prepared salad.
5. Top the salad with some roasted pumpkin seeds and melon seeds.
6. This delicious salad is now all yours to devour!

Egg Salad

  • Type

    Non Veg

  • Persons

    1
Ingredients:
2 boiled eggs mashed or chopped, half an onion finely chopped,1-2 garlic cloves finely chopped, herbs, chopped coriander leaves, 1 chilli finely chopped, half cup hung curd and a pinch of salt.

Method :

1. Boil the eggs in a container with water full till the eggs are submerged until you see cracks on the eggs.
2. After 15mins remove the eggs, put them in a bowl containing cold water for a while then remove the shells.
3. Chop the eggs into tiny cubes and add them into a mixing bowl.
4. Add chopped onions, garlic, herbs and some finely chopped chillies.
5. Add hung curd along with some salt according to your taste.
6. Serve the creamy egg salad over toasted bread or some roti.

Beet Pasta

  • Type

    Veg

  • Persons

    2
Ingredients:

12 oz pasta, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1 cup raw cashews boiled for 5 minutes, 2 cubed fresh roasted beets, 1 head garlic, 2 tbsp lemon juice, 1/2 cup milk, 1.5 cups pasta water, 1 tsp salt and pepper and goat cheese for garnish

Method :

1. Preheat oven to 450 F. Prep baking sheet with parchment.
2. Add beets and 1 head of garlic cut in half in baking sheet with olive oil and a sprinkle of salt and pepper. Bake for 25 minutes.
3. In a blender, blend cashews, beets, garlic (squeeze cloves out), lemon juice, pasta water, soy milk, salt and pepper. Blend until smooth.
4. Drain pasta then pour back into the pot and mix with the sauce.
5. Garnish with vegan feta or goat cheese, basil or parsley, and toasted pine nuts.
6. Enjoy the prettiest pasta you'll ever eat.

Thai Rice Noodles

  • Type

    Non Veg

  • Persons

    2
Ingredients:

1/4 pkt rice noodles, 500ml boiling hot water.

For the dressing : 1 tbsp dark soy sauce, 1 tbsp light soy sauce, 1 tbsp chopped garlic, 2 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sesame oil, 1 tsp chili flakes, 2 tbsps sugar and 3 tbsp fish sauce

Other ingredients : 1 1/2 cup sliced purple cabbage, 1 cup julienne carrots, 1/4 cup sliced spring onions whites, 1/2 cup chopped spring onion greens, 1/2 cup cucumber diced, 1/2 cup Boiled Sprouts, 1/2 cup crushed salted peanuts and 15-20 coriander leaves.

Method :

1. Take the rice noodles in a bowl & pour in the hot boiling water, let the noodles soften, and soak for 10-15 mins.
2. While the noodles soak, chop and cut all the vegetables & keep them ready.
3. Next, prepare the dressing; mix all the ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
4. Drain the rice noodles and start assembling the salad. Store ingredients in individual containers and assemble the desired quantity when you're ready to eat.
5. In a bowl, add the noodles and veggies, drizzle with the decoding,and toss well.
6. Always dress the salad just before serving for that extra crunch. Serve on a plate & garnish with more peanuts. Crunch, munch, and enjoy!

Broccoli Chicken

  • Type

    Non Veg

  • Persons

    2
Ingredients:

For the poaching liquid : 3 cups water, 2-3 tsp salt, 1 tbsp coriander seeds + 10-12 black peppercorns 10-12 black peppercorns (crushed), 1 tsp garlic powder, tsp dried oregano, juice of 1 lemon, 250 gm Broccoli and 2 chicken breasts

For the dressing : 1/2 cup whisked yogurt, 2-3 tbsp light soy sauce, 2 tbsp mustard sauce, salt to taste, 1/2 cup whisked yogurt, 2-3 tbsp light soy sauce, 2 tbsp mustard sauce,salt to taste,juice of half a lemon,1/2 tsp sugar

Other ingredients : 1 cup boiled fox tail millets, 2 tbsp roasted pumpkin seeds and feta cheese.

Method :

1. Wash and cut the broccoli florets; then wash and set aside the chicken breasts.
2. In a sauce pot, combine water, salt, coriander seeds, black peppercorns, garlic powder, dried oregano, and lemon juice for the poaching liquid.
3. Next, prepare the dressing; mix all the ingredients in a bowl and set aside. Adjust salt for a salty and sour broth. Bring to a boil and blanch the broccoli for 2-3 mins, then drain.
4. For the millets, wash and soak them for 15 mins, then boil in salted water and drain.
5. Mix all dressing ingredients in a bowl and set aside.
6. In a large bowl, combine chopped broccoli, chicken, millets, and dressing. Mix well and serve!

Baked Potato Salad

  • Type

    Veg

  • Persons

    2
Ingredients:

For the roasted potatoes: 250gm baby potatoes, 2-3 tbsp olive oil, 2 tsp garlic powder or chopped, 2 tsp onion powder, 2 tsp chilli flakes, 1 1/2 tsp Kashmiri chilli powder, Salt to taste, 1/2 tsp crushed black pepper, For the dressing, 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice or white wine vinegar, Salt and pepper to taste

For the dressing : 2 tbsp olive oil, 2 tbsp lemon juice or white wine vinegar, Salt and pepper to taste

Other ingredients : 1/4 cup sliced onions, 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, 5-6 green and black olive, 1/4 cup chopped dill leaves, 5-6 boiled cauliflower florets, 5-6 boiled broccoli florets, 1-2 tbsp feta cheese

Method :

1. Wash the baby potatoes and cut into half, take all the ingredients for the marination in a boil, mix well and toss the potatoes in it.
2. Transfer this to baking tray and roast or air fry for 8-10 mins at 190C.
3. While the potatoes are roasting, cut and prep all the other veggies.
4. Now prepare the dressing in a bowl, just whisk together all the ingredients and keep it ready, when the potatoes are ready, just add everything to the bowl, toss it well and serve.
